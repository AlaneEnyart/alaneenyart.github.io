Alane EnyArt

"I've been absolutely terrified every moment of my life — and I've never let it keep me from a single thing that I wanted to do." — Georgia O'Keeffe

My paintings emerge from layered acrylic motion — shifting textures and accidental forms that suggest leaves, water, and imagined botanical structures.

What makes my work truly unique: I use no brushes or tools of any kind. Each piece is created on loose canvas by pouring paint in layers, building depth and a living quality that no brush could achieve. When complete, every work is gallery wrapped and ready to hang.

My greatest inspiration is the breathtaking beauty of the Gulf of Mexico — its luminous skies, ever-shifting water, and the way light transforms everything it touches.

🏆 Second Place — Alliance of the Arts, Fort Myers All Florida Show, 2024 · "Hibiscus Blooming" · Nearly 700 entries

My goal with every piece is to give its owner something deeply personal — a creation that reflects their own love of life and the world around them.

My art business has been designated a "Trusted Art Seller" with The Art Storefronts Organization — so you can shop with complete confidence, knowing I stand fully behind the quality and value of every piece I create.
